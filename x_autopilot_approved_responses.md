## 🚫 What I Hate (DO NOT GENERATE THIS)
* **Generic "AI-style" roasts:** 
  * *Example:* "spend 3 hours tweaking v1 of my AI track and it still sounds like my cat's squeaky toy. bump the spectral balance to 1.5, genius."

* **Overly polished advice:** Avoid anything that sounds like a tech tutorial, blog post, or helpful assistant trying to teach.
