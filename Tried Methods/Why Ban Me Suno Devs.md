## Overview

Notes on account access patterns and recovery experiences with the Suno platform.

## Account Recovery Observations

- Account access can be temporarily restricted following certain usage patterns
- Access may be restored after a cooling-off period
- Secondary accounts may provide continuity during primary account restrictions
- Account status can change without explicit notification

## Practical Takeaways

1. Maintain backup account(s) for continuity
2. Export/save important generations regularly
3. Document account status changes with timestamps
4. Be aware that platform ToS may evolve and affect account standing
