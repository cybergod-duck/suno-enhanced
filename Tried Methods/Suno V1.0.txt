
SUNO-101

Prompt Length:
- Limit prompts to 4-8 lines for best results.
- Use the Continue feature to create longer songs by combining shorter clips.

Newlines:
- Format your song by putting each line on its own line.
- Add an extra blank line between verse and chorus.

Punctuation:
- Omit punctuation at the end of lines for best results.
- Use brackets for metatags and parentheses for ad libs.

SUNO-201

Metatags:
- Use metatags in moderation when describing the style of music.
- Avoid artist-specific metatags.
- Add the 'vocals' tag for lyrics meant to be sung.

Song Structure Metatags:
- Use '[Verse]' and '[Chorus]' metatags.
- Add a newline between sections for clarity.

Ad Libs:
- Use ad libs, like '(woo)', in moderation.
- Ad libs are typically added at the end of lines.

Extra Credit

Non-Lexical Vocables:
- Experiment with non-lexical vocables like 'la la la' to extend prompts.
